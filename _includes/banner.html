DC Code & Coffee

This group meets every other week on Sundays at either U.S. News & World Report (map) headquarters in Georgetown, or General Assembly (map) headquarters in downtown DC. Developers of all skill levels come together to work on and discuss projects with each other. You may also be interested in our sister groups NoVa Code & Coffee, Alexandria Code & Coffee, Centreville Code & Coffee, and Baltimore Code & Coffee.
